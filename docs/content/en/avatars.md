# Character Avatars

> Guide to configuring character visual representation: static images, GIF animations, interactive Live2D, and 3D VRM models.

In Soul of Waifu, your companion is more than just text on a screen. You can give them a physical presence that reacts to the context of your conversation. The program automatically analyzes every message generated by the AI, detects the emotions, and instantly updates the avatar's expression, pose, or gesture.

---

## How the Emotional Classifier Works

The program reads the text of the model's response and maps it into one of **28 distinct emotional states**:

| | | Emotions in Soul of Waifu | | |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| `neutral` | `joy` | `love` | `sadness` | `anger` |
| `admiration` | `amusement` | `annoyance` | `approval` | `caring` |
| `confusion` | `curiosity` | `desire` | `disappointment` | `disapproval` |
| `disgust` | `embarrassment` | `excitement` | `fear` | `gratitude` |
| `grief` | `nervousness` | `optimism` | `pride` | `realization` |
| `relief` | `remorse` | `surprise` | | |

You can configure a unique visual reaction for each of these emotional states.

## Character Visualization Methods

You can choose one of four visual formats depending on your preferences and computer specs:

::: cards
#### <i class="fa-regular fa-image"></i> 1. Images & GIF Animations
The lightest option, which puts absolutely zero load on your CPU and graphics card.
*   **How to configure:** Create a folder on your computer named after your character. Place your images (`.png`/`.jpg`) or animations (`.gif`) inside, renaming each file strictly after its corresponding emotion (e.g., `neutral.png`, `joy.gif`, `anger.png`).
*   **Where to store:** Move the folder containing the image pack to the `Soul-of-Waifu/assets/emotions/images/` directory.
---
#### <i class="fa-solid fa-wand-magic-sparkles"></i> 2. Interactive Live2D Models
An interactive 2D animation technology that deforms flat image layers, creating a smooth effect of physical depth, breathing, and motion.
*   **Motions and LipSync in v2.4.0:** The new version introduces full support for motion animations. The companion can blink, move their arms, and change poses. The model's mouth automatically opens in sync with the TTS voice (LipSync).
*   **Where to find & store:** Download a pre-compiled **moc3** model (legacy *moc* format is not supported) from gaming repositories on GitHub. Place the folder containing the model files into the `Soul-of-Waifu/assets/emotions/live2d/` directory.
---
#### <i class="fa-solid fa-cube"></i> 3. 3D VRM Avatars
An open format for 3D avatars. VRM models are fully three-dimensional: they support free rotation in space, realistic hair/cloth physics, and highly expressive facial blendshapes.
*   **How to create:** You can easily design your own unique avatar using the free **VRoid Studio** software or download one of thousands of community creations from the **VRoid Hub** platform.
*   **Where to store:** Place the `.vrm` model file into the `Soul-of-Waifu/assets/emotions/vrm/` directory.
:::

## Step-by-Step Avatar Installation

![Avatar selection in editor](assets/sc_avatars_1.png)
*Editor Interface: The avatar selection field in the right-side configuration panel*

1.  Ensure your avatar files are placed in the correct directories inside `assets/emotions/`.
2.  Open the **Expressions Selector** of an existing character on the main screen.
3.  In the left panel, select the visualization type (Images / Live2D / VRM).
4.  Choose your model folder or file from the dropdown list.
5.  Click the save button.

![Character in chat](../assets/screenshots/sc_screenshots_chat.png)
*The companion is displayed on the right side of the chat window and reacts in real-time*

Now open a chat with your character. During your conversation, the avatar will appear on the right side of the screen, softly animating, blinking, and dynamically shifting facial expressions based on the AI's generated replies.

::: warning
**Troubleshooting Blank/Black Screen:**
If the program freezes when loading a Live2D or VRM model, simply wait a bit and leave the program open. If there's no button to open the Expressions Selector, make sure the Soul of Waifu System module is enabled in the program settings.
:::
