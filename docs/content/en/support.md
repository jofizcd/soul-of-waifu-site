# Support the Author

> Project history, our shared achievements, and ways to financially support the development of Soul of Waifu.

**Soul of Waifu** is the result of my three years of continuous work on the project. What started in 2023 as a simple and fun console utility for personal use has today grown into a fully-fledged, independent local platform for interacting with virtual characters in a single ecosystem.

---

## The Path We Have Walked Together

During active development, the project went through a major evolution:
*   An active and large community of artificial intelligence roleplay enthusiasts was formed.
*   Support for running local language models directly in the application was introduced.
*   The concept of a complete ecosystem of virtual characters was realized, with a gradual transition from text chat to a desktop companion with access to the real world.
*   It became possible to use all cutting-edge technologies "out of the box" without needing extensive knowledge in the field of AI.
*   An integrated local model management center, Models Hub, was implemented with Hugging Face database integration.
*   An innovative long-term memory system, Soul Memory, was created, featuring personal character diaries and logic self-healing.
*   An interactive RPG mode, Soul Stage, was launched under the control of an AI Game Master.

::: tip
**What seemed like a dream is now a reality:**
In the early stages of the project, I could only plan to create "a smart desktop companion that can see your screen and react to actions in real-time." Now, in version v2.4.0, this highly complex module (Soul Companion) with built-in computer vision, neurohormones, and MCP protocol support is fully written and ready to run on your PC!
:::

Soul of Waifu is a true lifesaver in a world where most similar AI software requires deep technical knowledge, command-line skills, and programming languages from the user. My goal is to give you a powerful tool for working with AI "out of the box", packaged in a beautiful and ergonomic interface.
