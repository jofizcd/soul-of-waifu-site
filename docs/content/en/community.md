# Community

> Our Discord community, Telegram channel, and YouTube: platforms where you can share characters, find like-minded people, and participate in the project's development.

The community is the beating heart of **Soul of Waifu**. It is thanks to our active users that the project continues to evolve, and the roleplay experience becomes deeper and more diverse. Like-minded people gather here to share their creations, help newcomers, and discuss modern AI technologies.

---

## What is Discussed in Our Community?

::: cards
#### <i class="fas fa-id-card"></i> Character Cards
Authors share their unique characters in the V2 Spec PNG card format. You will find many ready-made characters—ranging from fantasy heroes to helpful desktop assistants.
---
#### <i class="fas fa-terminal"></i> Prompts & Lorebooks
Members share configured system prompt presets, test advanced sampler settings (Min-P, DRY, XTC), and upload huge lorebook worlds for playing in Soul Stage mode.
---
#### <i class="fas fa-puzzle-piece"></i> MCP Plugins & Mods
Developers from the community can upload their Python scripts to expand the capabilities of the Desktop Companion (integration with smart homes, system utilities, and third-party APIs).
---
#### <i class="fas fa-handshake-angle"></i> Technical Help
If you encounter an error while installing PyTorch, can't enable CUDA acceleration, or if your local server crashes—you will always find advice and help with log analysis in Discord.
:::

---

## Shape the Future of the Project

Since Soul of Waifu is a project that thrives on the activity of its community, your participation directly determines its path of development:

*   **Suggest Ideas:** There are dedicated feedback and suggestion channels in Discord. The most interesting ideas from users are regularly implemented by the author in the program code.
*   **Participate in Tests:** Active community members get access to closed alpha and beta testing of future major program updates.
*   **Write Guides:** You can help other users by writing instructions on creating the perfect lorebook or selecting optimal quantization for low-end graphics cards.

::: warning
**Important Rules of Conduct:**
Our community is built on principles of friendliness, mutual help, and respect for others' work. Please be polite to other members and follow the Discord server rules. Violators of the rules are quickly and permanently banned.
:::
