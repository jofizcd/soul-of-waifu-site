Soul Memory — The Thread of Existence

Every character in Soul of Waifu possesses a single, unified digital brain. Whether you are chatting over voice, embarking on an RPG quest, or working with your companion on the desktop, they share the exact same memories, relationship states, and personal reflections. Their soul is not fragmented — it evolves continuously across all environments.

  • Subjective Diary: Deep first-person journal entries reflecting on your interactions across all modes
  • Persistent Relationship: Trust, affection, and behavioral traits adapt globally and persist seamlessly
  • Memory Self-Healing: A background agent constantly resolves factual and logical contradictions
  • Cognitive Profiles: Four distinct modes of memory depth to optimize your system resources

Features

Everything you need for immersion

Powerful engine under the hood, intuitive interface on top

Ergonomics & Focus

The interface is designed to remove distractions, keeping the spotlight entirely on your companion. With a streamlined sidebar, organized tabs, and polished animations, navigating the platform is natural and effortless right from the start.

  • Clean and ergonomic visual style
  • Sidebar and clean tab-based layout
  • Interactive chat with smooth, flowing text animations
  • Laconic notification toasts that never interrupt your immersion

Freedom of AI Choice

Run open-source models completely offline or connect to leading cloud APIs. With the built-in server, setting up local GGUF models is seamless — simply download your preferred model directly from the interface.

  • Top-tier cloud models: Claude, DeepSeek, GPT-4o, Gemini, Grok, and Qwen
  • Built-in Llama CPP Server with full hardware acceleration
  • Visual download manager for local models with instant cancellation
  • Native acceleration for CUDA, Vulkan, Intel SYCL, and AMD HIP

Feature-Rich Chat

A complete space for deep conversation: manage chat histories, organize your library into folders, make live audio calls, and set custom backgrounds. You can even send empty messages to prompt your companion to continue their train of thought.

  • Organize your character library easily using custom folders
  • Seamless history import/export and contextual author notes
  • Local web client to chat from your smartphone within your home network
  • Custom chat backdrops and a dedicated text-generation stop button

Voice & Visual Presence

Characters speak naturally using advanced speech engines with life-like inflections. Voice playback starts instantly, sentence-by-sentence, eliminating awkward wait times. The platform supports dynamic Live2D (with motion animations), 3D VRM avatars, custom images, and GIFs.

  • Instant, sentence-by-sentence voice streaming with zero delays
  • Multiple text-to-speech options, voice cloning, and RVC parameters
  • Interactive Live2D and VRM avatars with motion support and LipSync
  • Real-time text analysis recognizing 28 distinct emotional states

Professional Narrative

Fine-tune story arcs and character depth using layered lorebooks, custom state variables, and the interactive Soul Stage mode. The virtual Game Master takes care of all the world-building, tracking, and campaign progression for your adventure.

  • Link multiple lorebooks simultaneously with semantic story triggers
  • Soul Stage RPG system with a unified World State and inventory
  • Custom state variables visualized via an adaptive horizontal HUD
  • Industry-standard V2 Spec compatibility (PNG/JSON card import)

Advanced Configuration

A complete control center for your AI engine. Fine-tune advanced text samplers, local model quantization parameters, MoE layers, context window sizing, and enable Thinking Mode for reasoning networks.

  • Advanced samplers: Min-P, XTC, DRY, and dynamic temperature
  • Low-level LLM tuning: and custom launch settings
  • A wide variety of Soul of Waifu feature expansion modules
  • Total appearance customization: unique themes and message bubbles

Built from scratch

A full platform rewrite (v2.0): new GUI, local GGUF models, Mistral AI integration, and XTTS voice synthesis. A month later, v2.1 added OpenAI and OpenRouter support plus Edge TTS with RVC voice cloning.
